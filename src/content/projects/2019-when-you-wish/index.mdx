Tuacahn Amphitheatre · Outdoor stage · May 1 – October 24, 2019.

A jukebox revue spanning the Disney songbook — Pocahontas, Tarzan,
Snow White, Beauty and the Beast, Aladdin, Moana, Mulan, Mary Poppins,
and more. Featured cast included Alize Cruz (Moana), Josh Strickland
(Tarzan / Hercules), Lindsay Roginski (Mary Poppins), Patrick Nowak
(Aladdin), and Dallyn Vail Bayles (Beast).
